LearnSpace Group Study Rooms



LearnSpace offers 10 study rooms and 2 conference rooms available at Learnspace.


  • Only registered students, staff and faculty members with approved access may book study or conference rooms.
  • Rooms can last up to 4 hours per day.
  • There is a maximum of one reservation per day, 2 reservations per week, Monday to Sunday.
  • Bookings can be made up to 10 days in advance.
