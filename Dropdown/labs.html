LearnSpace Lab Facilities



There are 2 laboratories available for each of the following departments: Chemistry, Physics, Computer and Robotics


Please follow our booking and safety procedures.


  • Only registered students, staff and faculty members with approved access may book laboratories.
  • Undergraduate Students must have a supervising professor or lab technician present during the entirety of the booking.
  • Lab Bookings can last up to 2 hours
  • There is a maximum of one reservation per day and 1 reservations per week, Monday to Sunday.
  • Labs operate from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm
  • You must submit your booking at least 48 hours in advance.
  • You must read the Laboratory Safety Code and complete the Laboratory Safety Quiz before submitting a booking
