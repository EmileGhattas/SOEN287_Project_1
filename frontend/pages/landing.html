FAQs

How do I book a room?

You can book rooms through the Bookings tab above. Select the type of room, date, and time slot, then confirm your request.

Who can use the lab facilities?

Lab facilities are available for registered students and staff with approved access.

How long do my bookings last?

For both rooms and labs the booking lasts at maximum 4 hours, and you can only book one room a day.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, cancellations can be made up to 24 hours before your reserved time through your account page.
